FAQ

How does PoetryCam work?

You take a photo, choose a style, and the app generates a poem inspired by the image with the help of artificial intelligence.

Are photos saved on the device?

Yes. Photos are saved in the device gallery. When you log in, content is also stored in our services so you can retrieve it if the app is uninstalled.

Does PoetryCam work without an internet connection?

An internet connection is required to generate the poem. Viewing already saved content can work offline.

Is the app free?

PoetryCam is free with ads. You can remove ads with a one-time purchase.

How can I delete my account?

You can delete your account from the app settings. Alternatively, email us and we'll help with the request.

Can I report an issue or share feedback?

Yes. Send an email with a brief description of the issue, your iOS version and app version, and any helpful details.
